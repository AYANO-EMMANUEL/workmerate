State Internal Revenue Services Across Nigeria

Nigeria's tax system includes both federal and state-level tax authorities. While the Nigeria Revenue Service (NRS) oversees federal tax administration, each state has its own Internal Revenue Service (IRS) responsible for administering, assessing, and collecting taxes and internally generated revenue within that state.

Visit Your State IRS Portal

What Are State Internal Revenue Services?

State Internal Revenue Services are statutory agencies established by state governments to assess, collect, and manage taxes and other revenues due to the state. They ensure tax compliance, provide taxpayer services, issue tax clearance certificates, and often support economic planning and development at the state level. These agencies typically handle:

  • ✱
    PAYE and Withholding Taxes in state.
  • ✱
    Stamp Duties and other levies
  • ✱
    Internally Generated Revenue (IGR) reporting
  • ✱
    Taxpayer education and compliance assistance

What You Can Do on State IRS Sites

State IRS websites generally allow residents and businesses to:

Register for tax identification (state TIN where applicable)

Calculate and pay taxes online

File tax returns or obtain tax clearance certificates

Download forms, guides, and tax schedules

Access taxpayer support and contact information

Verify tax documentation
